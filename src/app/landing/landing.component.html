Independent Communication Awareness Network


About

The purpose of the ICAN system is to give a leadership team of an organization a method of communicating and organizing itself with or without the use of the electric grid and cellular network. The ICAN system can send out forms to its members to collect information during an emergency, which will be collected by the ICAN system and used to populate various reports. Allowing leadership to organize and direct a response as quickly as possible.

Technology

During emergencies when electric power and cellular towers are still functioning, the ICAN system will utilize standard texting services to contact all members of the organization. When the electric grid and cellular networks are down, the ICAN system will utilize a mesh network of private single user antennas made by GoTenna.
